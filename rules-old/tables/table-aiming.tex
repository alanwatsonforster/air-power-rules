\begin{tablenote}{0.7\linewidth}
\begin{itemize}
\item \changedin{1C}{1C-apj-36-errata}{Always round down fractions.}{Use Table~\ref{table:fractions} when calculating {\onethird} and {\twothirds} of speed for the requirements for manual, computed, and advanced bomb sights and round down when calculating the requirements for ballistic bomb sights.}
\item Minimum aiming is at least 1 FP while on a LOA.
\item If full aiming not accomplished, apply a modifier of \plus{3} to the attack die roll.
\item If full aiming is accomplished, apply the bombsight modifier from the aircraft's ADC.
\itemaddedin{1C}{1C-apj-36-errata}{\changedin{2B}{2B-aiming-modifiers}{Do not combined these fractions with the {\onethird} or {\twothirds} for attaining bonus modifiers.}{FPs that count towards satisfying the aiming requirements do not count towards gaining aiming modifiers.}}
\end{itemize}
\end{tablenote}
