\textbf{\Large Cover Photograph}

An A-7A Corsair II of VA-174, “The Argonauts,” prepares to launch from USS \textit{Ranger} during the Vietnam War. The aircraft is armed for an Iron Hand mission, with AGM-45 Shrike anti-radiation missiles, CBU-75 Sadeye cluster bombs, and AIM-9D Sidewinder air-to-air missiles. The photograph is undated, but is probably from late 1967 or 1968.
